Garrett Goodwin

Garrett Goodwin is a drummer from Robbinsville New Jersey. He has been playing drums since the age of four and can’t help but smile when behind a drum set. He is currently in his final semester at Berklee College of Music pursuing a degree in Drum Set Performance. Throughout his time at Berklee, Garrett made it a point to play with as many musicians as he could which landed him a variety of gigs ranging from funk, country, R&B, and musical theater. He built a reputation as someone who was versatile, reliable, and a blast to create music with. Not to mention his many drum faces that come out when performing. Garrett hopes he can help students access the pure joy that playing music can bring. To him, music has always been an outlet to escape the hardships of life.
