iPlay® is an innovative musical education program under the umbrella of Power Changes Lives, dedicated to introducing and teaching various musical instruments to children aged K-12 across the United States. Leveraging technology through the Microsoft Teams platform, iPlay® aims to create inclusive opportunities for children, particularly focusing on African-American youth, while welcoming all participants irrespective of their background. The program acknowledges the diminishing presence of arts education in public schools and strives to bridge this gap, ensuring that every child has the chance to experience the joy of music and instrument playing. iPlay® embodies a commitment to transforming lives through music education. By addressing the barriers to access, providing resources, and fostering a love for the arts, we aim to cultivate a vibrant community of young musicians equipped to navigate their educational journeys with enhanced creativity and confidence. Through this initiative, we believe we can inspire future generations to embrace their musical talents and realize their full potential.

Mission Statement

The mission of iPlay® is to ignite passion and love for music in children by providing access to musical instruments, fostering creativity, and enhancing learning capabilities through structured music education. By ensuring that all participants, particularly those from underserved communities, receive the necessary tools and instruction, we aim to empower the next generation of musicians and leaders.

Program Goals

  • Access to Musical Education: Provide underprivileged children with musical instruments and professional instruction to promote inclusivity in the arts.
  • Enhanced Learning: Improve the cognitive and social skills of participants through structured musical learning, supported by research highlighting the benefits of music education.
  • Empowerment through Arts: Foster self-esteem and confidence in children, enabling them to express themselves creatively through music.

    • Program Objectives

  • Instrument Provision: Supply each participant with a high-quality instrument, ensuring they have the tools necessary for effective learning.
  • Instructor Support: Fund qualified instructors’ stipends through internship programs, fostering professional development while ensuring quality education for our students.
  • Structured Learning Environment: Organize classes in small groups (max 10 students), facilitating personalized instruction and peer interaction to enhance the learning experience.
  • Flexible Learning Model: Conduct classes remotely via Microsoft Teams, allowing accessibility for children in diverse geographical locations across the country.
  • Community Engagement: Foster collaborations with local communities and schools to increase awareness and participant enrollment.
  • Ongoing Evaluation: Implement an assessment framework to monitor participants’ progress and ensure program effectiveness, adapting curricula as needed to meet learning objectives.

    • Curriculum Overview

    The iPlay® curriculum will focus on a range of musical instruments, including but not limited to:

  • Piano
  • Guitar
  • Drums
  • Voice
  • Saxophone
  • Clarinet

    • The curriculum is designed for all skill levels, including beginners with no prior experience.

    Class Format

  • Frequency: Classes are conducted twice a week, for a period of 12 weeks.
  • Class Size: A maximum of 10 students per class to ensure individual attention and a supportive environment.
  • Session Duration: Each class will last for 60 minutes, allowing adequate time for instruction and practice.

    Technology Utilization

    By utilizing the Microsoft Teams platform, iPlay® can extend its reach and create a virtual classroom environment where students can learn from qualified instructors regardless of their geographic location. This technology also allows for recording lessons, providing resources for further practice.
