Senior Connect®

Keeping Our Seniors Connected – Every Wellness Check, Every Meal Shared and Tech-Savvy Moment Matters

One of our key initiatives is the Senior Connect® Program, a comprehensive wellness initiative managed by POWER. This program offers a variety of essential services, including physician checkups, disease management with medication support, and nutritional programs aimed at combating food insecurity. Additionally, Senior Connect® integrates technology into its services by providing participants with Kindle Fire tablets. These devices enhance accessibility by allowing seniors to connect easily with their primary healthcare providers, order medications, and facilitate wellness checks by family members. Through these efforts, we aim to create a supportive and connected community for our seniors, enhancing their quality of life and independence.

Testimonials

Seniors Imapacted

Mrs. Johnson, a 78-year-old widow living alone, had previously struggled to access regular healthcare due to limited mobility and transportation difficulties. Through our telehealth program, she now receives monthly check-ups from her physician, significantly improving her health management and reducing her anxieties about her health. The remote monitoring system also alerts her doctor to any irregularities in her blood pressure, preventing potential health crises. Her quality of life has demonstrably improved, and she feels more secure and in control of her health.

Mr. Lee, an 85-year-old diabetic, was initially hesitant to use technology. However, through our digital literacy training, he not only learned to use his glucose monitoring device and transmit the data to his doctor but also mastered video conferencing, allowing him to connect regularly with his family. This increased interaction has a positive impact on his mental and emotional wellbeing, demonstrating the broader societal benefits of bridging the digital divide. These successes are not isolated incidents; they represent the broader transformative effect of our program.
