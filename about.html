Dedicated to serving low-income, minority, and underserved communities, POWER strives to create meaningful and lasting impact.

Our Mission

POWER is dedicated to uplifting and empowering low-income, minority, and underserved communities by providing essential resources and support in health, housing, education, food security, and social justice. Through innovative programs and strategic partnerships, we work to break the cycle of poverty, promote equity, and create sustainable opportunities for individuals and families in need.

Our mission is to ensure that every person has access to the tools and services necessary to lead a healthier, more stable, and fulfilling life. By fostering community-driven solutions and advocating for systemic change, we strive to build a more just and compassionate society for all.

Our Vision

At POWER, we envision a world where every individual, regardless of their background or circumstances, has access to the essential resources needed to thrive. We are committed to breaking down barriers and creating opportunities that empower low-income, minority, and underserved communities.

Through our initiatives in health, housing, education, food security, social justice, and community wellness, we strive to foster sustainable change. Our goal is to not only address immediate needs but to create long-term solutions that uplift individuals and families, ensuring a future where everyone has the chance to succeed.

Our History

POWER was founded in 2010 to directly serve community needs, launching initiatives such as Project F.E.E.D.® (Friends Ensuring Every Dinner), Project Warmth®, and iLearn® under a 501(c)(3) sponsorship.

On September 10, 2018, POWER officially obtained its 501(c)(3) designation from the Internal Revenue Service. Following this milestone, POWER expanded its services to focus on aging, education, finance, food insecurity, health, housing wellness, and social justice reform in low-income, minority, and underserved communities (LIMUs). This growth introduced new programs, including The Justice League®, Senior Connect®, iAmArt®, iPlay®, and LavaLove®.

Through these initiatives, POWER continues to uplift and empower communities, ensuring that essential resources and opportunities reach those who need them most. With a steadfast commitment to service and equity, POWER remains dedicated to creating lasting change and a brighter future for all.
