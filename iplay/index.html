Mission Statement

The mission of iPlayÂ® is to ignite passion and love for music in children by providing access to musical instruments, fostering creativity, and enhancing learning capabilities through structured music education. By ensuring that all participants, particularly those from underserved communities, receive the necessary tools and instruction, we aim to empower the next generation of musicians and leaders.



Program Goals

1. Access to Musical Education:
Provide underprivileged children with musical instruments and professional instruction to promote inclusivity in the arts.
2. Enhanced Learning:
Improve the cognitive and social skills of participants through structured musical learning, supported by research highlighting the benefits of music education.
3. Empowerment through Arts:
Foster self-esteem and confidence in children, enabling them to express themselves creatively through music.

Curriculum Overview

The iPlayÂ® curriculum will focus on a range of musical instruments, including but not limited to:

- Piano
- Guitar
- Drums
- Voice

The curriculum will be designed to cater to a variety of skill levels, ensuring inclusivity for children with no prior musical experience.

Class Format

Frequency:
Classes are conducted twice a week, for a period of 12 weeks.
Class Size:
A maximum of 10 students per class to ensure individual attention and a supportive environment.
Session Duration:
Each class will last for 60 minutes, allowing adequate time for instruction and practice.
